# Commercial Readiness

Project: AntiQuotar
Commercial status: not commercialized

## Position

AntiQuotar is currently a local helper tool. It is not prepared for sale, subscription billing, account management, or customer support workflows.

| Area | Requirement | Status |
| --- | --- | --- |

| Support | Define support channel and response expectations | todo |

## Điều cần người phụ trách kiểm tra

- Whether AntiQuotar is only a personal/local helper or should become a packaged product.
- Upstream license and attribution requirements.
- Any privacy implications from quota/account workflows.
- Support expectations if shared with other users.

## Current Rule

Do not add payment code, telemetry, account systems, or licensing enforcement unless the user explicitly asks to commercialize the project.

## Handoff cho Codex

- Keep commercialization work on hold.
- Do not add payments, licensing, telemetry, or customer accounts.
- Record license findings before any packaging or public sharing work.
